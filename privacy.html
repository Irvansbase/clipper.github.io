Privacy Policy for Clipper

Last Updated: January 8, 2025

1. Introduction

Clipper ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application to create and upload video clips.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Google Account Information: Email address and basic profile information obtained through Google OAuth 2.0 authentication
  • Content Data: Video content, titles, descriptions, and tags that you choose to upload through our application

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Video Processing Data: Temporary video files and transcripts processed locally on your device
  • API Usage Data: Information about your interactions with platform APIs for upload purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and authorize access to your account
  • Content Upload: To upload video clips to your connected platform
  • Content Generation: To use AI (DeepSeek API) to analyze transcripts and generate optimized titles, descriptions, and tags
  • Service Improvement: To improve the functionality and user experience of our application

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

  • All video processing occurs locally on your device
  • Authentication tokens are stored locally in encrypted format (token.pickle)
  • No video content is stored on our servers

4.2 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google APIs: For authentication and content upload functionality
  • DeepSeek AI API: For content analysis and metadata generation
  • yt-dlp: For video downloading and processing

4.3 Security Measures

  • OAuth 2.0 protocol for secure authentication
  • All API communications are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Authentication credentials are stored locally and never transmitted to our servers

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Store your video content on external servers
  • Access your account beyond the scope you authorize

We MAY share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or valid legal process
  • Service Providers: With AI providers (transcripts only) and platform services (for uploads)

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Deletion: Delete data by removing token.pickle
  • Revocation: Revoke access via your connected account permissions
  • Opt-out: Stop using the application anytime

7. Platform API Services

Clipper uses third-party API services. By using this application, you agree to be bound by their respective terms and privacy policies.

8. Data Retention

  • Authentication Tokens: Stored locally until deleted or revoked
  • Processed Content: Deleted after upload unless saved
  • Transcripts: Stored locally until manually deleted

9. Children's Privacy

This application is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. International Data Transfers

Data may be processed in countries outside your residence with appropriate safeguards.
